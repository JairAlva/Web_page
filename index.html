Welcome to my website!

Subnational Politics, Electoral Behaviour, and Political Parties

I am Ph.D. student at Carlos III University in Madrid. I am interested in the study of individuals’ social and political behaviour in relation to their political and institutional contexts, with a particular focus on Latin America and Southern Europe.
